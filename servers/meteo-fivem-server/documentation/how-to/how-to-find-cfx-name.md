# How to Find Your CFX Name

Your CFX name is your username on the Cfx.re platform. We need this when setting up your server access. Here is how to find it.

***

## Steps

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
**Log in to CFX Portal**

Go to <a href="https://portal.cfx.re/" target="_blank">portal.cfx.re</a> and log in with your Cfx.re account.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
**Open the account menu**

On the top right side of the page, hover over the profile icon (the colored circle with your initial). A dropdown menu will appear.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
**Click Account**

Click the **Account** option from the dropdown. This opens your account settings page.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
**Find your username**

Under the **Profile** tab, look for the **USERNAME** field. The value shown there is your CFX name. Copy it and send it to us.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}
