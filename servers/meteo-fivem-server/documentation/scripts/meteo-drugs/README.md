# Meteo Drug System

This is a guide about testing the meteo fivem drug script designed exclusively for meteo server.

{% hint style="info" %}
get access to our exclusive video testing guide on Discord to see all of this in action.
{% endhint %}

***

## Before You Start

* Access to the meteo test server
* Know how to [spawn items](../../how-to-spawn-items.md)
* Familiar with [getting started](../../getting-started.md) basics

***

## Testing Coca and Weed Farming

### Getting Started

* get these items: `meteo_empty_baggy`, `meteo_pot`, `meteo_watering_can`, `meteo_fertilizer`, `meteo_weed_seed`, `meteo_coca_seed`
* also you can get these items from admin menu items section or just use /giveitem
* lets go to farm location. there are more locations. you can add more from config once you get them too and also turn off blips if needed. currently they are turned off
* lets go to `/tp 2830.2139, 688.2671, 41.9126`

### Planting and Growing

* use the `meteo_coca_seed` and place the plant
* point target and add water and fertilize the plant
* also check growth and maintain
* do the same for the weed seed too and harvest the plant

***

## Testing Weed Processing

* get `/giveitem yourid meteo_weedtable 1`
* normally players can get these things doing crime things, crafting and get from crates and also from the blackmarket
* you have to go to your apartment or any property you own and open the furnishing menu
* search for weed table and place it
* now get `/giveitem yourid rolling_paper 5`. normal players can get this from the smoke on the water shop
* now point target to the weed table and click on roll joints. it will give you a joint
* now lets try weed brick. make sure you have 10 `meteo_weed_buds` and point target and click on make weed brick
* now you can sell them. please check the testing guide of [meteo-drugselling](../meteo-drugselling/) for more info :)

***

## Testing Coca Processing

* make sure you have the `meteo_coke_table`, `meteo_cokemixer`
* as before go to any property you own and open furnishing menu and place both coke mixer and coke table. please make sure to put the coke table in correct side like the video. otherwise it will bring u to other side /:
* now point target to coke mixer and it will start process all coke leaf we harvested
* now make sure you have `meteo_empty_baggy`. player can get this from the smoke on the water shop
* then go to the table and point target and click on make coke baggy
* after finish you will get the bag of coke. and ready to sell. check out the [meteo-drugselling](../meteo-drugselling/) testing guide for more info

***

## Testing Meth Lab

* get following items. players can get them from crafting and also doing crime things and earn from their rewards and crates
* `meteo_pseudoephedrine` x3, `meteo_acetone` x2, `meteo_lithium` x1 and `meteo_meth_lab`
* now go to your property you own. lets do the apartment. now open furnishing menu like we did before and search meth lab kit and place it
* point the target and click on look inside. put all meteo\_pseudoephedrine x3, meteo\_acetone x2, meteo\_lithium inside it. these item count is for one recipe
* now click start cooking
* after that click on heat control and control the heat and learn :) be careful wrong heat and blow up
* if everything goes well. after finish cooking you can look inside and get meth
* can you sell this too. check out [meteo-drugselling](../meteo-drugselling/) for more info

***

## Good to Know

* all farm locations, growth times, processing recipes and drug items are configurable on our config. you can change them once you get the server. we will guide you :)
* connected with [meteo-drugselling](../meteo-drugselling/) for selling, [meteo-craftingtables](../meteo-craftingtables/) for crafting drug tables, and crime system for obtaining materials
